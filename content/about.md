I'm **Estyak Ahamed**, a mechanical engineering undergraduate at the
**Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET)**, class of 2026.
Before BUET I studied at Notre Dame College, Dhaka.

Most of my work sits where mechanical systems meet software. I lead the
**Autonomy & Software** and **Drone** sub-teams at [Team
Interplanetar](https://github.com/khajababa1139), BUET's Mars rover team —
building the rover's perception pipeline, SLAM stack (ROS, RViz, Gazebo),
Nav2-based autonomous pathfinding, and the embedded layer: CAN bus motor
control, RTK GPS, multi-sensor fusion, and dual-link comms on an NVIDIA
Jetson Orin. The team was a finalist at the **Anatolian Rover Challenge 2025**.

Outside robotics I build with deep learning (PyTorch, TensorFlow, JAX) and
full-stack web (ReactJS, Node.js, PostgreSQL), and I do simulation work in
Ansys and LAMMPS. The [projects on the home page](/) cover the range.

This site is where I document it all — daily, when I can. Each entry is a
concrete problem and what actually fixed it, so someone else hitting the same
wall can find it through [search](/search/).
